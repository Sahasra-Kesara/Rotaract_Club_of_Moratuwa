About Us

  • Club Name - Rotaract Club of Moratuwa
  • Parent Club - Rotary Club of Moratuwa
  • Based Type - Community-Based
  • Chartered Date - 28th of January 2021


Rotaract Clubs are part of a Global effort to bring peace and international understanding to the world. It brings together people of the abovementioned age group to exchange ideas with leaders in the community and develop leadership and professional skills by having fun and enjoyment through service.

Rotaract focuses on the development of young adults as leaders in their communities and workplaces under 4 main avenues namely,

Club Service
Community Service
Professional Development
International Service
